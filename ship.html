Prepare for Launch

Congratulations! You've gotten through designing your own website. Now it's time to share it with the world.



Attracting Users

A finished website needs users. While your search optimization should attract users from search results pages, you can also use the Open Graph meta tags you created to accurately portray your site on social media.

Another good idea for attracting users to your website is writing an article about the topic and sharing it on popular blog sites like Medium. Include a link from your article to your website - you can also see how well the referrals from each blog are working using Google Analytics. Medium has built-in stats for your stories, which can be useful to determine which content is the most popular.

Your goal should be to get the most eyes as possible on your project. While this isn't a requirement for the course, it's in your best interest!
